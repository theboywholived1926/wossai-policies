Frequently asked questions

What is Woss AI?

Woss AI is your AI-powered weight loss, nutrition tracker, and health coach. You’ll receive weekly check-ins, personalized plans, and adaptive recommendations to help you achieve your health goals.

How do weekly plans work?

Every Monday, in your time zone, you can complete a check-in that updates your progress and generates a new plan tailored to your results. Activities are split into body and mind categories to help you stay lean and build better habits.

Can I try Woss AI without creating an account?

Yes. Woss AI offers a free 7-day trial during onboarding so you can experience the app before becoming a member.

How do subscriptions work?

Woss AI currently offers a monthly subscription for $14.99. The subscription includes a free trial, automatically renews, and can be canceled at any time through your App Store account settings.

What happens to my data if I delete my account?

All your personal data — including check-ins, plans, and progress — is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. Your data is only used to personalize your experience within Woss AI.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team by emailing team@wossai.app. We typically respond within 24–48 hours.
